About

We aim to spread the pure truths in the Bible and help people understand the essential truths about the second coming of the Lord, so that those who genuinely long for God’s appearance can successfully welcome His return.

Fellowship Topics

  • How the Lord Returns in the Last Days
  • Recognize God’s voice and Welcome the Lord
  • How to Discern the True Christ and False Ones
  • The Mystery of God’s Incarnation

How the Lord Returns in the Last Days

The Lord Jesus promised us His second coming and prophesied that His return would happen in two ways. So, in what way will the Lord come in the last days?

Recognize God’s voice and Welcome the Lord

God’s sheep hear His voice. When the Lord returns in the last days, how can we recognize God’s voice and welcome the Lord?

How to Discern the True Christ and False Ones

The Lord Jesus prophesied that during His second coming in the last days, there would be deceptions by false Christs. So, how can we discern the true Christ from false ones and successfully welcome the Lord?

The Mystery of God’s Incarnation

2000 years ago, the Lord Jesus appeared in the flesh to do His work. He prophesied that when He returns in the last days, He would incarnate again to appear and work as the Son of man. So, what is the incarnation? What mysteries are hidden within this?
